  • YSLaurent

    Welcome to Yslaurent Website, our website provides information about our stream and the contents of it.

    This is presented by our group, YSL. Our first upcoming podcast stream is about teenage stereotypes and we aim to tackle problems that are often encountered by teenagers by being generalized by the media, movies, tv shows and other people. and how it affects their way of thinking and how they react and act on certain things.

    Our group focuses mostly on topics regarding Teenagers and the Stereotypes that are branded to them, we want our audience to feel safe in sharing their opinions and stories to us.

    The creation of this whole website, what went down before this, during this or in the future as well as our upcoming first podcast stream are the first few steps that this group has taken into a journey that is much bigger than what we can imagine as of now.




  • Our Target Audience

    Additionally from the contents of the homepage, we want to share to you our target audience. The target audience of our stream definitely gives off the impression that we only strive for teenagers to tune into our podcasts but in reality, we are gladly open and definitely hoping to see a lot more people from different age groups as well. This is due to the fact that every one of any age can help and prevent these harmful stereotypes from getting into every one’s head, not just from teenagers as other people are subject to pushing said stereotypes.
